Audience participating media

An audience participating media.
Each audience goes into a room filled with darkness and flipped chair. The Audience goes through the memory of oppressed 12 years of korean education and the eventually induced attitude of life, contemplate about it, and write a short thought in the prepared notebook. The memory will accumulate, so will our depth of introspection.
