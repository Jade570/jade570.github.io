Music Project @ 2018 ATC

Aleatoric Jazz Instrument - Music by Code

An musical instrument that anyone-those who doesn't know anything about music-can make their own music. The user drives, and the song will be made by the behavior the user has made (how they turn the car wheel or push an accelerator).

Made with Unity, Processing, and Pure Data.
